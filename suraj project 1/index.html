About MITCORER

"MIT college"

MIT Group has launched “MIT College of Railway Engineering and Research” (MITCORER) at Barshi, Dist. Solapur. MIT Group's legacy in the field of revolutionary education inspired us to venture into the unique field of rail and transportation. MITCORER aspires to cultivate and train the students who will help/provide solutions to the greatest technological and social challenges of the 21st century. Our Vision is to create future leaders who would manage and lead powerful organisations in the emerging corporate landscape.
